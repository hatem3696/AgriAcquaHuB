Tilapia is a freshwater fish that is widely cultivated in Bangladesh, and is known for its firm texture and mild flavor. It is often used in fish balls, cutlets, and fries. It can grow up to 60 centimeters in length and weigh up to 5 kilograms. Tilapia is rich in protein and omega-3 fatty acids. It is often used in traditional Bengali cuisine. It is a highly nutritious fish and is recommended for a healthy diet. It is an important source of income for many fishermen in Bangladesh. It is often sold in markets across the country. Tilapia is a highly versatile fish that can be cooked in a variety of ways, including frying, grilling, and baking. It is a popular fish for export to other countries.
